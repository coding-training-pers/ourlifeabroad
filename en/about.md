## 🌍 Our Story

We're a French couple who said **screw it** to the 9-to-5 routine in 2018.

**The initial plan:** Go to Australia on a Working Holiday Visa for 1 year, explore the world, then return to France like good little citizens.

**Reality:** We stayed **7 years**. And we still haven't gone back to France. 😅

---

## 🎒 Our Philosophy: Slow Travel

**We're NOT typical backpackers.**

You'll never see us:
- ❌ Changing cities every 2 days
- ❌ Ticking off "Top 10 Things to Do" lists
- ❌ Eating at restaurants for every meal
- ❌ Pretending everything's perfect on Instagram

**What we actually do:**
- ✅ Stay **1-2 months per city**
- ✅ Rent apartments (not hotels)
- ✅ Cook, shop at local markets
- ✅ Work remotely (we're not trust fund babies)
- ✅ Share the **real struggles** and actual budgets

**Basically: we live normally, just... elsewhere.**

## 🗺️ Our Journey

### 2017-2025: Australia 🇦🇺
8 years of Aussie life. Sydney, Melbourne, Perth... We did everything: waiter, sales, office jobs, freelancing.

**Key takeaway:** Australia taught us to live differently. Less stress, more nature, better work-life balance.

### 2023: Back to Asia 🌏
Bali, Vietnam, Thailand, Philippines. Rediscovering Asia with a different perspective (and a better budget than at 20).

### 2024-2025: South America 🌎
Our current adventure. Argentina, Paraguay, Peru... And the journey continues in Colombia, Ecuador, Bolivia.

### 2026+: Back to Asia?
We're thinking of returning to Asia (Japan, South Korea, Taiwan...) but nothing's set. That's the beauty of it.
